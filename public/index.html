About Us

Our founders are cereal entrepreneurs themselves. As entrepreneurs we needed to build websites and apps to get our products to market, but we really struggled to find a company that had the expertise, security, proven track record and affordability to get us to where we needed to go.

Seeing that this was a real problem for entrepreneurs, we invented 'Deep Technology', so that other entrepreneurs could get the technology that they needed, and focus on what they do best. Running the business!

Comprehensive Service

High Quality

Our Software Engineers have years or even decades of experience in the industry. From the best aesthetics, to the fastest loading times: you are in safe hands.

Affordable Pricing & Tailored to Your Needs

We know that as an entrepreneur you are on a tight budget. That's why we strive to make our services: the most affordable in the industry. You will be amazed how cheaply we can build your business for you.

We will consider partial payment in equity, providing financing options and more if we believe in your business. We genuinely know how tough it is and, want to do what we can to help you achieve your dreams and your ambitions.

What do we develop

From advanced technology products, with complex algorithms and AI, to simple websites or eCommerce stores.

Because of the amount and quality of the developers that operate for us, there is really no project to big, too small, too complicated or too simple for us.

Our specialism is in working with Entrepreneurs. As entrepreneurs ourselves, we are able to empathise with you properly and tailor the service to your requirements.
